About me

Hello! My name is Darryl. I'm a video producer and writer interested in topics that make you go "I didn't know that", or "hmm that's interesting."

In my current job, I lead a team to produce explainers -- which as a Philosophy major I love making. I've made videos on topics ranging from International Relations to politics and social issues in Singapore, and also which fried chicken is the best in Singapore.

I am passionate about media and think a lot about how best to present a story.

This website was built from scratch using mainly Bootstrap. But I hope it will be get iteratively better along the way.
